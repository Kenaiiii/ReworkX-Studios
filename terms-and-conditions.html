ReworkX Studios — Terms of Service

These terms explain how our subscription works, what’s included, and what to expect when working with ReworkX Studios.

1. Service Overview

ReworkX Studios provides affordable, subscription-based web design services. For $12.99/month, you receive:

A custom-built website based on your business information

Unlimited minor content updates upon request

Optional paid add-ons (e.g., logo refresh, rush updates)

2. Free Preview Policy

Before subscribing, we offer 1 free homepage concept preview. This is created based on your business name and services. No payment is required to view or request revisions to your preview.

Final website delivery and publishing require active subscription payment.

3. Subscription Terms

Billing: Your subscription is billed monthly at $19.99 via via Stripe or any other secure payment provider used by ReworkX Studios.

Start Date: Your billing begins only once you approve your final site and complete payment.

Updates: Unlimited minor updates are included. These include text, image, link, or button changes.

Major redesigns or new feature builds may incur additional one-time fees (quoted upon request).

You may cancel anytime via email or through your client portal (if applicable).

No refunds are provided for previous billing periods.

Upon cancellation, your website will be unpublished automatically at the end of your billing cycle unless you request immediate removal or domain transfer.

Logos or design assets created through paid add-ons (e.g., logo refresh) are yours to keep.

Minor updates are typically completed within 1-2 business days.
